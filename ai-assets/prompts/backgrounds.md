## backgrounds/oceanografic-shell

Create a full-bleed cinematic background for the main Oceanogràfic Jumanji UI. The scene must feel like entering an underwater expedition inside a public aquarium: glass tunnels, deep blue water, light beams, drifting bubbles, coral silhouettes, subtle sonar rings and marine reflections. Composition: safe center area for title, form controls and primary actions; richer detail on edges and corners; dark enough for white text; no characters; no readable text. Placement: lobby, mission shell, route selection and general app background. Output: 1920x1080 desktop and 1080x1920 mobile, optimized for web as webp and png.
