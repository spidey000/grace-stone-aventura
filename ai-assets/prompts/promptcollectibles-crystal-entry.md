**Palette:**

## Grok Prompt

**Style:** Flat vector illustration, gem icon, cut glass crystal, premium collectible.
**Subject:** A single reward crystal: "Cristal de la Entrada" (Entry Crystal). A faceted teal-and-gold gem with a cut-glass geometric shape (diamond-like with angled facets). Inner warm glow from the center. Wet reflections on the facet edges. The crystal feels like a premium marine relic, not a generic gem.
**Palette:** Base teal `#0E8277`, gold `#F1C96B` inner glow, jade `#56D0B1` highlight, amber `#D89A2B` warm reflection.
**Mood:** Premium, rewarding, magical. A collectible you want to earn.
**Composition:** Centered crystal, strong silhouette, transparent background. No shadow touching the edge.
**Technical:** Clean vector shapes, faceted geometry, inner glow, transparent PNG.
