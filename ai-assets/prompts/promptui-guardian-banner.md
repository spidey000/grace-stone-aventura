# UI: Guardian Banner

## Style Context

**Visual direction:** Oceanogràfic Jumanji — marine expedition ribbon carved from glass and coral. Strong readable middle zone for copy. Decorative sonar pulses and shell fragments at ends. Materials: wet glass, coral, aged metal.

**Palette:**
- Deep background: `#04131D` | Elevated surface: `#1B5A6D`
- Ancient gold: `#F1C96B` | Amber: `#D89A2B`
- Jade: `#56D0B1` | Deep teal: `#0E8277`
- Primary text: `#F7F3EA`

**Rules:** No text. Wide banner. Strong readable center zone.

## Grok Prompt

**Style:** Flat vector illustration, wide ribbon banner, expedition-themed UI component.
**Subject:** A wide marine expedition ribbon banner for the riddle intro and treasure banner. The banner is carved from glass and coral: a dark teal body with gold and amber edges. The center zone is clear and flat for text overlay. The left and right ends are decorated with sonar pulse rings, shell fragments, and small coral growths. No text baked in.
**Palette:** Dark teal `#123B4E` body, gold `#F1C96B` and amber `#D89A2B` edge trim, jade `#56D0B1` for sonar ring accents, deep background `#04131D` for shadow depth.
**Mood:** Important, narrative, expedition announcement. Feels like a discovery reveal.
**Composition:** 1440x360 wide banner. Left and right decorative caps, clean center 60% for text. Compact 720x240 variant keeps same proportions.
**Technical:** No baked text. Masked or transparent background. Clean scalable shapes.

## Negative Prompt

no text, no readable letters, no watermark, no logo, no unreadable tiny detail, no flat generic UI, no neon sci-fi, no clutter, no photorealistic people

## Output

- Sizes: 1440x360, 720x240
- Format: PNG + WEBP
- Background: transparent or masked
