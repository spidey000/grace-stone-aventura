# Print: Passport Back Cover

## Style Context

**Visual direction:** Oceanogràfic Jumanji — back cover of printable passport. Subtle marine expedition frame, badge placeholder, final closing message area. Same visual language as front cover.

**Palette:**
- Deep background: `#04131D` | Elevated surface: `#1B5A6D`
- Ancient gold: `#F1C96B` | Text secondary: `#D6CBB9`

**Rules:** No text baked in. Breathing room. Not busy. A4 portrait.

## Grok Prompt

**Style:** Premium print design, book back cover, clean layout, vector illustration.
**Subject:** The back cover of the printable passport. A subtle marine expedition frame: thin gold border with small compass and wave motifs at the corners. A small badge placeholder circle at center-top. The rest of the page is calm negative space for a closing message or stamp. No busy surfaces — peaceful and complete.
**Palette:** Deep background `#04131D`, elevated `#1B5A6D` for the inner panel, gold `#F1C96B` for the frame and corner motifs at low opacity, text secondary `#D6CBB9` for subtle decorative lines.
**Mood:** Calm, complete, premium. A satisfying closing page.
**Composition:** A4 portrait 2480x3508. Simple frame, centered badge spot, generous negative space.
**Technical:** Print quality 300 DPI. No baked text. Minimal decoration.
