# Brand: App Icon Oceanogràfic

## Style Context

**Visual direction:** Oceanogràfic Jumanji — rounded-square app icon. Central eye-of-the-ocean or compass emblem, surrounded by glass, water and gold glow. Marine silhouette, no text. Must read at 48px. Materials: wet glass, aged metal, polished stone.

**Palette:**
- Deep background: `#04131D` | Elevated surface: `#1B5A6D`
- Ancient gold: `#F1C96B` | Amber: `#D89A2B`
- Jade: `#56D0B1` | Deep teal: `#0E8277`

**Rules:** No text. No watermark. Rounded-square composition. Works for PWA, favicon, maskable variants.

## Grok Prompt

**Style:** Premium flat vector icon, iOS-style rounded-square app icon, polished glossy finish.
**Subject:** A rounded-square app icon for the Oceanogràfic Jumanji experience. At center, a compass-like "eye of the ocean" emblem with a jade and gold gradient. The emblem is surrounded by a ring of wet glass with subtle refraction lines. A dark teal to deep navy gradient background. Soft gold glow radiates from behind the central emblem. A marine silhouette (stylized wave or dolphin arc) is embedded subtly. No text.
**Palette:** Base deep navy `#04131D` to teal `#123B4E` gradient. Central jade `#56D0B1` and gold `#F1C96B` emblem. Amber `#D89A2B` glow accents.
**Mood:** Premium, mysterious, adventurous. Feels like a real expedition app.
**Composition:** Centered symmetrical, strong focal point. The rounded-square frame is part of the design (not a mockup).
**Technical:** Clean vector shapes, smooth gradients, glossy subtle reflections. Rounded-square corners (iOS style). Transparent background outside the icon shape (for maskable variants).

## Negative Prompt

no text, no readable letters, no watermark, no logo, no random characters, no clutter, no photorealistic poster, no generic app icon, no flat boring design, no rough edges
