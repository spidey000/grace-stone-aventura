# UI: Final Panel Surface

## Style Context

**Visual direction:** Oceanogràfic Jumanji — luminous ceremonial panel. Deeper glow, more gold, more ocean light than the mission panel. Clear area for honor badge and final message. Materials: wet glass with gold inlay, polished sea stone.

**Palette:**
- Deep background: `#04131D` | Elevated surface: `#1B5A6D`
- Ancient gold: `#F1C96B` | Amber: `#D89A2B`
- Jade: `#56D0B1` | Coral: `#FF8A65`
- Primary text: `#F7F3EA`

**Rules:** No text. No logo. More luminous than mission panel. Decorated edges, never crowded.

## Grok Prompt

**Style:** Flat vector illustration, ceremonial UI panel, premium game completion surface.
**Subject:** A final screen panel surface for the completion view. More luminous and ceremonial than the mission panel: a deeper golden glow radiates from the center, ocean light cascades from above. The background is dark teal fading to a warm gold center. A clear area at center-top for the honor badge and a clean zone below for final message and action buttons. Edges are decorated with coral motifs, gold filigree, and subtle pearl inlays.
**Palette:** Base `#123B4E` warming to `#1B5A6D`, gold `#F1C96B` and amber `#D89A2B` glow, jade `#56D0B1` accents, coral `#FF8A65` for celebratory highlights.
**Mood:** Triumphant, warm, ceremonial. A reward screen that feels earned.
**Composition:** 1600x1200. Centered vertical layout, gold radial gradient behind content zone, decorated frame with breathing room.
**Technical:** No baked text. Subtle glow effect, clean vector shapes. Masked edges for overlay placement.

## Negative Prompt

no text, no readable letters, no watermark, no logo, no unreadable tiny detail, no flat generic UI, no neon sci-fi, no clutter, no photorealistic people, no dull dark mood

## Output

- Size: 1600x1200
- Format: PNG + WEBP
- Background: transparent or masked edges
