## Grok Prompt

**Style:** Flat vector illustration, gem icon, cut glass crystal, premium collectible.
**Subject:** A single reward crystal: "Cristal de los Misterios" (Mysteries Crystal). A faceted violet-marine gem with cut-glass geometric facets. Inner glow from the center. The color is mysterious — deep violet with marine blue undertones and a warm coral-pink inner glow, like an unknown deep-sea creature.
**Palette:** Base violet-marine, coral `#FF8A65` inner glow, deep teal `#0E8277` shadow facets, gold `#F1C96B` tiny sparkle accents.
**Mood:** Premium, rewarding, mysterious, magical.
**Composition:** Centered crystal, transparent background. No shadow touching the edge.
**Technical:** Clean vector shapes, faceted geometry, inner glow.

no text, no readable letters, no watermark, no logo, no clutter, no generic game loot, no flat emoji style, no noisy background, no shadow on canvas edge
