# Collectible: Crystal — Oceanic Islands

**Palette:**
- Muted sand and seaweed
- Text secondary: `#D6CBB9` | Deep teal: `#0E8277`

**Rules:** No text. Transparent background. One crystal only.

## Grok Prompt

**Style:** Flat vector illustration, gem icon, cut glass crystal, premium collectible.
**Subject:** A single reward crystal: "Cristal de las Islas Oceánicas" (Oceanic Islands Crystal). A faceted muted sand-and-seaweed gem with cut-glass geometric facets. Inner glow from the center. The palette is desaturated — sand beige meeting deep seaweed teal, like remote oceanic islands.
**Palette:** Base muted sand `#D6CBB9` at 60% saturation, deep teal `#0E8277` shadow facets, seaweed green `#1B5A6D` for depth, soft gold `#F1C96B` inner glow at low opacity.
**Mood:** Premium, rewarding, remote, mysterious.
**Composition:** Centered crystal, transparent background. No shadow touching the edge.
**Technical:** Clean vector shapes, faceted geometry, inner glow.
