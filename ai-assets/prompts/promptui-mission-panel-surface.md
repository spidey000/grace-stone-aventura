# UI: Mission Panel Surface

## Style Context

**Visual direction:** Oceanogràfic Jumanji — carved marine control board, glass-reinforced expedition panel. Dark teal frame, subtle gold edging, wet reflections. Materials: wet glass, aged metal, sea stone, resin.

**Palette:**
- Deep background: `#04131D` | Elevated surface: `#1B5A6D`
- Ancient gold: `#F1C96B` | Amber: `#D89A2B`
- Jade: `#56D0B1` | Deep teal: `#0E8277`
- Primary text: `#F7F3EA`

**Rules:** No text. No logo. Large panel surface with readable interior zone. Heavier corners.

## Grok Prompt

**Style:** Flat vector illustration, UI surface design, game panel aesthetic, marine expedition theme.
**Subject:** A large mission panel surface for the route screen. A carved marine control board made of dark teal reinforced glass with subtle gold edging. Wet reflections on the surface. A readable interior zone (center 60%) is clean and flat for station copy and challenge controls. The corners are heavier and more decorated with sonar rings, coral filigree, and nautical rivets.
**Palette:** Base dark teal `#123B4E`, frame edge `#1B5A6D`, gold trim `#F1C96B` at 30% opacity, jade `#56D0B1` accent dots, deep background `#04131D` for shadowed edges.
**Mood:** Sturdy, expedition-ready, premium UI component.
**Composition:** 1600x1200 large panel. Stronger border on all four sides, clean center. Rounded inner corners.
**Technical:** No baked text. Masked or transparent edges for flexible placement. Subtle texture overlay for glass feel.

## Negative Prompt

no text, no readable letters, no watermark, no logo, no unreadable tiny detail, no flat generic UI, no neon sci-fi, no clutter, no photorealistic people

## Output

- Size: 1600x1200
- Format: PNG + WEBP
- Background: transparent or masked edges
