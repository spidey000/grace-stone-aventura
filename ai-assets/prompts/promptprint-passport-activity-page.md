# Print: Passport Activity Page

## Style Context

**Visual direction:** Oceanogràfic Jumanji — printable activity page. Route checklist, small boxes or circles for stamping, calm oceanographic decorative margins. Materials: nautical parchment, aged paper.

**Palette:**
- Mid background: `#082635` | Elevated surface: `#1B5A6D`
- Ancient gold: `#F1C96B` | Jade: `#56D0B1`

**Rules:** No text baked in. Clear center for handwriting and stickers. A4 portrait.

## Grok Prompt

**Style:** Print layout design, activity page, clean checklist format, vector illustration.
**Subject:** A printable activity page for the passport. A route checklist with 18 small circular stamping boxes arranged vertically in two columns. Each box has a tiny sonar ring or compass indicator next to it. Calm oceanographic decorative elements in the margins: subtle seaweed, bubbles, coral dust, faint map contour lines. The center is kept clear for handwriting.
**Palette:** Mid background `#082635` parchment, elevated `#1B5A6D` for the stamp boxes, gold `#F1C96B` for decorative margin elements at low opacity, jade `#56D0B1` for accent dots.
**Mood:** Clean, printable, expedition log. Invites interaction.
**Composition:** A4 portrait 2480x3508. Two-column checklist layout, decorated margins, wide center.
**Technical:** Print quality 300 DPI. No baked text. Clear handwriting zones.
