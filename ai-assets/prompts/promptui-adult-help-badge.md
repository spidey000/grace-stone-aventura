# UI: Adult Help Badge

## Style Context

**Visual direction:** Oceanogràfic Jumanji — helpful expedition marker. Life ring, lantern, beacon, guardian cue in marine glass style. Fits inside a button with text. Legible at 24px.

**Palette:**
- Elevated surface: `#1B5A6D` | Ancient gold: `#F1C96B`
- Jade: `#56D0B1` | Coral: `#FF8A65`
- Soft danger: `#E05C52` | Ice: `#BFEFFF`

**Rules:** No text. Transparent background. Small icon badge. Helpful tone, not warning.

## Grok Prompt

**Style:** Flat vector icon, minimalist UI glyph, marine expedition beacon, helpful marker.
**Subject:** A small icon badge for the adult help button. A marine-style life ring or guardian beacon: a ring shape with alternating teal and gold segments, with a small star or compass arrow at center. The icon is round with a subtle glass sheen. Friendly and helpful, not alarming. No text.
**Palette:** Base gold `#F1C96B` and teal `#0E8277` for the ring bands, jade `#56D0B1` for the center, coral `#FF8A65` or soft danger `#E05C52` at low opacity for alert-but-friendly cue.
**Mood:** Helpful, supportive, guiding. A beacon you want to press.
**Composition:** 256x256 master, centered. Reads clearly at 24px. Transparent background.
**Technical:** Clean vector shapes, smooth gradients, glass-like highlight. Fits inside a text button.

## Negative Prompt

no text, no readable letters, no watermark, no logo, no clutter, no photorealistic, no flat generic UI circle, no scary or warning symbols, no red cross

- Master: 256x256
- Additional: 64x64, 32x32
