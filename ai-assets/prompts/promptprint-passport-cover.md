# Print: Passport Cover

## Style Context

**Visual direction:** Oceanogràfic Jumanji — premium field journal from a marine expedition. Deep blue-green base, gold trim, central badge space. A4 portrait print quality. Materials: aged nautical parchment, leather-like texture, gold foil.

**Palette:**
- Deep background: `#04131D` | Elevated surface: `#1B5A6D`
- Ancient gold: `#F1C96B` | Amber: `#D89A2B`
- Jade: `#56D0B1` | Primary text: `#F7F3EA`

**Rules:** No text baked in. Title zone and name line area kept clear. Print-safe resolution.

## Grok Prompt

**Style:** Premium print design, expedition journal cover, vector illustration, book cover quality.
**Subject:** The front cover of a printable adventure passport for the Oceanogràfic Jumanji route. A deep blue-green base with the texture of aged nautical leather. Gold trim border with wave and compass motifs. A central badge placeholder (circular recessed area). A title zone at the top third with calm negative space. A name line area at the bottom. Coral and shell corner decorations.
**Palette:** Deep blue-green `#04131D` base, gold `#F1C96B` and amber `#D89A2B` border trim, jade `#56D0B1` accent dots, elevated `#1B5A6D` for inner panel.
**Mood:** Premium, expedition-worthy, mysterious but inviting.
**Composition:** A4 portrait 2480x3508. Centered layout, strong border, clear zones for title and name.
**Technical:** Print quality 300 DPI. No baked text. Gold foil effect on trim. Clean negative space.

## Negative Prompt

no text, no readable letters, no watermark, no logo, no clutter, no photorealistic people, no generic school worksheet, no photo collage

## Output

- Size: 2480x3508 px (A4 portrait, 300 DPI)
- Format: PNG + WEBP
- Background: dark textured cover
