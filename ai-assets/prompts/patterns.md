## patterns/ocean-floor-tile

Create a seamless tileable pattern for the Oceanogràfic Jumanji UI. The pattern must feel like a calm ocean floor texture that can sit behind cards and margins without fighting the UI: subtle bubbles, sonar arcs, contour lines, coral dust, soft glass reflections, eelgrass, shell fragments and faint map tracings. Keep the contrast low and the motion implied rather than explicit. The tile must loop perfectly on every edge. Placement: panel backgrounds, passport margins, loading states and decorative surfaces. Output: 1024x1024, webp and png.
